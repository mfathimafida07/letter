For My Kindi 🤍
March 2026

My dearest Kindi,

I never imagined that the first friend I met in TKM would turn out to be one of my best friends there.

From 2019 to 2026, we've come such a long way. You were always there beside me through all my highs and lows, ups and downs. You handled my situations in ways I still don't know how to. Not just mine, but for so many others too, you've always been such a strong emotional support.

✦    ✦    ✦

For others, you may be the "strong, independent, powerful" Dilutha who takes on every responsibility without hesitation. But for us, you'll always be our Kindi 🤍

I still remember that day during SIP when you were so excited to become the team leader, but didn't get the chance just because your phone didn't have WhatsApp. And then seeing your journey till final year, becoming the program committee main, steps main, I was silently watching everything like an introvert in the gallery, feeling so proud of my friend.

All those times when me and Hannapi used to talk about Favas and Junaidikka, you would always join in with your "unrealistic" expectations about your future partner. And now look at you, everything has fallen into place, and you've finally found your forever after all that "research" 🥹

✦    ✦    ✦

I don't even know how to express what I'm feeling right now. My Kindi is stepping into one of the most beautiful chapters of her life, the one she always dreamed of.

You were there for me at every moment, especially during my nikkah. I truly, really wish I could be there beside you now. I held onto hope till the very last moment that I'd somehow make it to your big day.

I'll be waiting to see the most vibrant, cheerful bride in all the photos that are yet to come ✨

Happy Dilsha Mubarak, Kindiii 🤍 — Fi
